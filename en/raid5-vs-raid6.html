RAID 5 vs RAID 6: Which One Should You Choose?

RAID 5 and RAID 6 are popular choices for NAS and servers. This guide explains the key differences so you can choose the right one.

Key Difference

  • RAID 5: Can tolerate 1 disk failure
  • RAID 6: Can tolerate 2 disk failures

Capacity Comparison

  • RAID 5 → (N - 1) × disk size
  • RAID 6 → (N - 2) × disk size

Learn RAID capacity calculation →

Performance

RAID 5 is generally faster for writes because it uses less parity. RAID 6 provides better safety but slightly slower write performance.

Which Should You Choose?

  • Choose RAID 5 → Better capacity efficiency
  • Choose RAID 6 → Higher data safety
