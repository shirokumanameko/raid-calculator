RAID Capacity Calculator

Enter disk count, disk size, and RAID level to calculate usable capacity instantly.

* Assumes identical disk sizes.
* Actual usable capacity may vary slightly.

How RAID Capacity is Calculated

  • RAID 0: Total capacity of all disks
  • RAID 1: Capacity of one disk
  • RAID 5: (N - 1) × disk size
  • RAID 6: (N - 2) × disk size
  • RAID 10: (N / 2) × disk size

Important Note

RAID is not a backup solution. It protects against disk failure, but not against accidental deletion, malware, or disasters.
