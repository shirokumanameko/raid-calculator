Best RAID for NAS: Which RAID Level Should You Use?

Choosing the right RAID level for your NAS depends on your priorities: capacity, performance, or data safety.

RAID Options for NAS

  • RAID 1: Simple and safe
  • RAID 5: Good balance
  • RAID 6: Higher fault tolerance
  • RAID 10: Performance + safety

Recommendations

  • Home NAS → RAID 1 or RAID 5
  • Large storage → RAID 5
  • Critical data → RAID 6
  • High performance → RAID 10
